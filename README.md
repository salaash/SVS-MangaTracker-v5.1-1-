## The problem

You read from five different sites. You never remember what chapter you're on. You open a title and have to scroll through to figure out where you left off.

S.V.S Manga Tracker fixes that. Paste any chapter link and it does the rest.

---

## What it does

- Paste a chapter URL from any manga or manhwa site
- Automatically pulls the title, chapter number, and cover image
- Saves your full reading list sorted by most recently opened
- Shows last opened time next to every title
- Click any entry to jump straight back to where you were
- Confirms before removing anything from your list

---

## Supported sites

Works out of the box on MangaDex, MangaKakalot, MangaNato, Webtoons, Asura Scans, Flame Scans, Reaper Scans, Toonily, MangaSee, Bato.to, MangaRead, and most other manga and manhwa sites. Falls back to a smart URL parser for anything else.

---

## How to install

1. Download the zip from this repo and extract it
2. Open Chrome and go to `chrome://extensions`
3. Turn on **Developer mode** in the top right
4. Click **Load unpacked** and select the extracted folder
5. The extension appears in your toolbar, ready to use

---

## How to use

1. Open a chapter on any manga site
2. Copy the URL
3. Click the S.V.S icon in your toolbar
4. Paste the link and hit **Add**
5. Confirm the title and chapter, then save

Your list updates automatically every time you open an entry.
